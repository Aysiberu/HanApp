Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 10 2025

Thank you for using HanApp. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you register and use our platform.

By using HanApp, creating an account, or accessing our services, you agree to follow this Privacy Policy.

1. Introduction

HanApp is a home service booking platform connecting customers with service providers. To deliver our services, we collect certain personal information from both customers and service providers.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

When registering as a customer or service provider, we collect: Full name, address, phone number, email address, zip code, and password.

For service providers, we also collect:

Your profession (e.g., plumbing, electronics, cleaning), skills, and optional service-related documents.

b. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the app, we may collect device information, IP address, usage data, and cookies or tracking information.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you register for an account, update your profile, book or accept a job, contact support, or use the HanApp platform.

4. Purpose of Collecting Information

Your information helps us: create accounts, match customers with service providers, process bookings, communicate updates, improve services, ensure safety, and provide technical support.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to operate and improve HanApp, verify accounts, enable bookings, process payments, and prevent fraud or misuse of the platform.

6. Sharing or Disclosure of Information

a. Between Users — Customer details may be shared with service providers for job purposes, and vice-versa.

b. Third-Party Services — We may use payment processors, cloud hosting, and analytics tools.

c. Legal Requirements — We may share data when required by law or to ensure user safety.

We do not sell your personal information.

7. Data Retention

We keep your information while your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize your data unless we are required by law to keep certain records.

8. Data Protection and Security Measures

We protect your data using encryption, secured servers, access controls, and regular security checks. However, no system is completely secure, and we encourage users to protect their login information.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, correct, or delete your information at any time.

You may also withdraw consent or request information about how your data is used.
